Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

Holistic Lifting ("the App") is developed by Sean Lu. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

Holistic Lifting does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party. The App has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

Data Storage

All data you create in the App — including your exercises, schedules, workout sessions, and settings — is stored locally on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, synced to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is accessible only to you and is governed by Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy.

Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (subscriptions and a lifetime option) processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive or store any payment information. Purchase history and subscription status are managed by Apple.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact holisticliftingapp@gmail.com.
